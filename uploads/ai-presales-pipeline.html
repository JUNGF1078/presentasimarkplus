Framework 02 · Scoring

The Spotlight Quadrant — Fit × Intent

Claude MCP scores every lead on ICP fit (segment, size, budget signal) and intent (downloads, demo request, repeat visits). Where they land decides the motion.

INTENT →
Warm the stageHigh fit, low intent → nurture w/ personalized content until a signal fires.
◎ SpotlightHigh fit, high intent → senior AE now. (Bio Farma, Telkom)
ParkLow fit, low intent → automated nurture or disqualify.
EducateLow fit, high intent → light-touch / self-serve.
FIT →

How the score is built

Fit = firmographics from Apollo + 6-segment classifier. Intent = behavioral trail (e-book, LP form, opens, pricing views). Weighted 0–100.

Why it matters

Human attention is the scarce resource. Route it top-right; automation holds the rest warm — no AE burns a day on a Park lead.

Output

A ranked board: each lead tagged Spotlight / Warm / Educate / Park, with the next best action attached.

Framework 03 · Qualification

P.L.A.Y. qualification + the 6-Segment Router

A Spotlight lead becomes an Opportunity only when it clears P.L.A.Y. — a game-native BANT. Score each 0–2; ≥6 advances.

P

Pain proven

A real, named problem surfaced in discovery — not a vague "interested".

L

Level of authority

Budget line exists & the DMU is mapped (who signs, who blocks).

A

Access

We can reach the decision unit through a champion or entry point.

Y

Yes-by date

A trigger or deadline — tender, academic calendar, campaign, fiscal year.

PUBLICAny approved tool
INTERNALAny approved tool
CONFIDENTIALEnterprise-approved (DPA) · training OFF · client data, proposals, code
RESTRICTEDInternal AI Agent System only · never external

HARD NEVERS
